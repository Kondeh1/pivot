# Privacy Policy for Pivot

**Last updated:** December 29, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information **Pivot** collects and how we use it.

### 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** When you sign up via Google OAuth, we receive your email address and basic profile name.
- **Task Data:** We store the tasks and descriptions you create within the app to provide the service.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect basic technical data (like browser type) to improve app performance.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain your to-do lists.
- To authenticate your identity securely.
- We **never** sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing.

### 3. Data Retention and Deletion
- We keep your data as long as your account is active.
- You can delete your tasks at any time. To fully delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at [Your Support Email/GitHub Issue].

### 4. Security
We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) to protect your data in transit.
